How Namco Works

Namco allows people to find cars anywhere and to rent them for as long as they want. Instead of having to use rental services and other means of transportation, our users have the ability and flexiblity to find a car that fits their needs.

Rent A Car

With Namco, you can find a car anywhere. Search for cars based off our available locations and choose a car based off of price/day and the model of the car.

List A Car

Listing a car with Namco is a breeze. You select the price per day as well as where your car can and cannot be picked up (custom location, your home, airport). List your car and get paid fast.
